## Concept
An automated podcast of Philip's stories (the ad-world / career stories he loves to tell), produced end-to-end with AI:
1. **Voice clone** — Philip's voice cloned (e.g. ElevenLabs) so episodes can be narrated in his voice.
2. **Virtual interview** — a bot interviews Philip to draw out each story.
3. **Script generation** — the interview is turned into a produced episode script.
4. **Auto-production** — script → cloned-voice audio → finished episode. _Voilà._
