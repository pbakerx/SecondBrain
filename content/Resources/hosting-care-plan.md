# Managed Hosting & Care Plan (Vercel)

_Productized recurring-revenue offering. One flat plan, easy to sell and manage._

**Offer:** **$75/month** flat — or **$750/year** (save ~$150, ~2 months free).
**Positioning:** "Your site, hosted fast and kept fresh — handled." A worry-free managed plan for small-business and portfolio sites.

---

## What's included (Hosting + Maintenance)
- **Hosting on Vercel** — fast global CDN, automatic HTTPS/SSL, Git-based auto-deploys with instant rollback.
- **Domain & DNS management** — setup, renewals-coordination, SSL, DNS changes.
- **Uptime & monitoring** — 99.9%+ target, proactive checks.
- **Contact-form handling** — delivery, spam protection, and routing to the client's inbox/CRM.
- **Monthly maintenance** — up to **1 hour** of content edits/small updates per month (text, images, links, hours, pricing). Non-cumulative.
- **Security & dependency updates** — keep the site patched and current.
- **Basic analytics** — monthly traffic snapshot on request.
- **Backups** — full Git version history; any version restorable.
- **Support** — response within **2 business days**.

## Not included (billed separately)
- New pages, features, or redesigns beyond the monthly hour.
- E-commerce, custom apps, integrations, or heavy development.
- Photo/video production, copywriting, SEO campaigns, paid media.
- Extra edit time beyond the included hour → **$95/hr** (or a higher-touch plan).

## Terms
- Month-to-month or annual; 30 days' notice to cancel. Annual paid upfront.
- Site build/setup is a **separate one-time project fee**; this plan starts at launch.
- Client owns their content and domain; code hosted in Philip's Git/Vercel account.

---

## Internal economics (not client-facing)
- **Cost basis:** Vercel **Pro = $20/mo for one team seat**, which hosts *many* small static client sites (Brock-type sites use negligible bandwidth/functions). Marginal cost per additional small site ≈ **$0** until high traffic.
- **Margin:** At $75/mo/client, gross margin is ~100% minus your maintenance time. **One client covers the entire Pro seat**; every client after is nearly pure margin.
- **Note:** Client/commercial sites must be on **Pro** (Hobby is non-commercial only). Host all client sites under one agency Pro team.
- **Scale math:** 10 clients = **$750/mo** ($9,000/yr) recurring on a ~$20/mo hosting cost + your edit time.

## Sell-it lines
- "Fast, secure hosting plus a monthly tune-up — so your site never goes stale or breaks."
- "One flat monthly fee. No surprise hosting bills, no 'who do I call' when something needs changing."
